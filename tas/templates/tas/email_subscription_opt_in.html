Welcome to Chameleon – we’re excited to have you join us!

Before you start experimenting there are a few important things we ask you to do:

  1. Sign up to the outages@chameleoncloud.org mailing list: this is the best way to find out about Chameleon outages – if you don’t keep track of this information you risk having them impact your experiments. An alternative way of finding out about outages is to look at the outages tab in your dashboard – however, with email notifications you don’t need to keep checking the website.
  2. Sign up to the users@chameleoncloud.org mailing list: this is where all user discussions (including users helping users) take place and is the best place to find out any news about the system.

We’d also like to draw your attention to the user icon in the top right corner of www.chameleoncloud.org: once you’re added to a Chameleon project, it provides a shortcut to the Chameleon GUI via the experiment option. If at any point you need help please submit a ticket via the help desk. And now the fun begins: click on our getting started guide and give it a whirl!

Happy experimenting, we are looking forward to working with you!
